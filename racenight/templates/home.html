How to play

Pick your horse, place your bets and watch the race!

Rules & Procedures:

  • Tickets are used to place bets
  • Each ticket is worth 50p
  • There will be five races, each with eight horses
  • First race starts at approximately 20:15, then every 15mins thereafter
  • You can bet on as many horses as you like provided you have enough tickets
  • There is a two minute countdown before each race begins
  • Betting will close approximately 1 minute before the race starts
  • Races are streamed 'live' via YouTube
  • After the race is over, the payouts are calculatedt
  • Payout per winning ticket is the number of tickets bet on the race divided by the number of winning tickets
  • If you've picked the winner then your account is automatically updated with your payout
  • Details of the amount bet on each horse for each race will be available to view after the race is over
  • After the last race is over your tickets will be converted back into cash and you'll receive your payout from your Race Night rep :-)
  • Remember it's just for fun!
